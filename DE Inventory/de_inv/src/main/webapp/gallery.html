Training of ACMO (Stores) / Chief Pharmacists / Pharmacists (Stores) - 06th and 07th December, 2018.

UPMSCL conducted a two day training workshop from 6th-7th December for all ACMO (Stores) and Chief Pharmacists / Pharmacists (Stores) from all 75 districts of Uttar Pradesh. The theme of the training workshop was- “Standard Operative Procedure for Quality Control of Drugs” and was held at UPHSSP, Conference Hall, Indira Nagar, Lucknow. The sessions were conducted to ensure the proper training of ACMO (stores) and Pharmacists and were based on topics like Online Indenting, Guidelines for supply of Drugs and Ensuring the Quality of Medicines. UPMSCL plans to conduct such informative sessions and workshops in near future to increase efficiency and growth.
