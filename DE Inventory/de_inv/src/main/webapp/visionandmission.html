Vision and Mission

UPMSC MISSION STATEMENT

UPMSC is a health care organization committed to deliver excellent health care for one and all by providing best of medicines and services. Our mission is to improve the health of the people and we serve with a commitment to excellence in all that we do.

Our goal is to offer quality care and services that set community standards, exceed patients’ expectations and the services are provided in a caring, convenient, cost-effective and accessible manner. To help the people in the state and deliver medical aid to people affected by conflict, epidemics, disasters or exclusion from health care.

VISION

Uttar Pradesh is a diverse state with 75 districts in it. With a huge population of over 200 million, it becomes difficult to ensure the availability of essential drugs and consumables everywhere across the state. UPMSC intends to work to fulfill these needs. It tries to meet the dynamic needs of state health. It strives to maintain the adequate availability of drugs, equipments and other consumables at warehouses by obtaining best procurement procedures.

VALUES

In pursuit of our mission, we believe the following are essential:

INTEGRITY-

We adhere to an uncompromising code of ethics that emphasizes complete honesty, transparency and sincerity. We seek to do the right thing always and everywhere in our day to day functioning.

EXCELLENCE-

We continually strive for excellence. We never stop learning and working to improve our skills, programs and services.

TRANSPARENCY-

We are committed to being truthful about the work we do - our successes and our failures. We seek to learn from every experience on how to improve.

SPEED AND AGILITY-

Developments in healthcare are fast paced. Prompt response to changing needs is our top priority and we are committed to ensuring that our services continue to be available to the needy at all times.

TEAMWORK-

We value team success more than individual one. Our mission and goals are achieved only by working together. We pool in diverse talents, backgrounds, ideas and experiences of our staff and through their collective efforts we aim to create services and solutions for the benefit of all.
